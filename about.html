About

HomeVisualize is a small business started in 2022 and based in Eagle Mountain, Utah. Sarah--the owner, founder, and only current employee--is a college student with aspirations to earn a bachelor's degree in Architecture from UVU after she finishes a bachelor's degree in Software Engineering at Snow Collge. Sarah is passionate about homesteading, self-sufficiency, and learning. She taught herself how to use the professional-level architectural software Home Designer Pro by building a replica of the 4,600-square-foot home she grew up in and has also taken a personal interest in learning about passive solar home design, optimization of floorplan layouts, and the basics of architecture in her spare time. Having been an artist ever since she was little, Sarah brings her strengths at visual design to the table to make sure you can end up with a custom home design that you love.
